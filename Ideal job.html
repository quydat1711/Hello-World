My Ideal Job

This page is about the job that I would want to work in the future. This can be changed and does not mean that I only have interest in this particular field.

What is required

  • Have experience working in network systems, connections
  • Must have the ability to thrive as a group and have great engagement with team members
  • Have the eager to learn more about different fields, seek for most updated data and information
  • Must have critical thinking, problem solving and time management skill
  • Familiar with using the Atlassian Suite (Jira and Confluence)
  • Have strong verbal and communication skills.

    • What I curently have

  • Critical thinking and problem solving skills
  • Ability to work in a group and collaborating ideas from different points of view
  • Adequate verbal and communication skills
  • Have the eager to learn and thrive

    • My plan to achieve the skills required

    The most important part is that I need to have the technical knowledge in this field in order to work in this position. To achieve this, I need to choose my major in Security and Cloud Computing at RMIT and during the learning process, find extra materials to broaden my knowledge in different fields. On top of that, I also need to improve my soft skills including: Pitching skills, critical thinking, project management and communication skills.

    If possible, I would search for intern positions in this field to gain experience and knowledge alongside my study. After graduating from RMIT, I need to find jobs for beginner level in this field and gain several years of experience before I apply for this position. Last but not least, I will keep myself updated with new technology and learning different things as I go to find what suits me best.
