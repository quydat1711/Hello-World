My Personal Profile

This is the results of the online tests I took that somewhat reveals my personality. Let's take a ride!

What these numbers mean to me

After seeing the results, I was surprised by how accurate the tests are. As I go through the explanation of each test, almost every aspects of it applies to me and some yet to be discovered. From the tests, I now have a better Idea about what suits me best, and from that, I can focus on what works for me to reach my full potential. As for the part that I have not discovered (or do not know that I have), I will keep experimenting and developing to fulfill myself and maybe someday I will find the missing parts!

Will these results influence my behaviour in a team?

As I mentioned before, the results apply to me really well and I'm happy with who I am and how I work in a team so that will not have a big influence on my behavior when doing teamwork. This will however make me feel a little more conscious about my behavior as a teammate and adjust accordingly to function best.

Will these results influence how I choose my teamates?

This result would not influence much on how I choose my teammates because I believe that anyone with a good attitude towards studying and is willing to work as a team can work together and figure their own way of getting the job done. Moreover, the results accuracy may differ from students to students so I think it's best to leave out the numbers and go the conventional way :)
